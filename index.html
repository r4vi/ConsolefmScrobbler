ConsoleFmScrobbler by gabek by r4vi

Scrobbles to Last.FM what is being played on the social music service Console.FM

If you listen to music people are playing on Console.FM now you can make those tracks count in Last.FM. This is a simple Google Chrome/Chromium extension that allows such functionality.

Dependencies

Google Chrome

Install

Open the extension file (consolefmscrobbler.crx) in chrome and click "Continue" when it asks if it's ok. Launch http://console.fm. A new tab will open asking you to authenticate your Last.FM account, do so. Refresh your console.fm tab.
